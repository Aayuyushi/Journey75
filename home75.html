If you’re active on social media, you may have seen the hashtag #75Hard at some point. We’re here to help you make it happen. We are here to assist you and support you as you complete your transformation challenge.




Rules to Follow

The 75 Hard Challenge is made up of six rules which have to be completed for 75 days straight. Skipping any one on any day is ‘non-negotiable’.

RULE 1

STICK TO A DIET


Choose a diet and commit to it for 75 days and do not deviate. You can set your own restrictions, but stick to it. It could involve eliminating processed foods, sugars, or other specific dietary restrictions.
Choose a diet with the goal in mind to improve your health and physique ... and something that will challenge you. The entire point of 75 HARD is to challenge you.

RULE 2

EXERCISE EVERYDAY


Complete two workouts, each at least 45 minutes long. One of the two workouts must be outdoors, it can be jogging, running, skipping, a sport, yoga anything.

It might be hard to squeeze in two workouts a day for some people, so a relaxation of completing one workout for atleast 1 hour 30 minutes is allowed from time to time.

Rest days are not allowed in this chellenge. You have to work out everyday for 75 days.

RULE 3

DRINK 4L WATER


Drink 1 gallon, i.e. approximately 4 litres of water everyday without fail.

Bodily fluids that assist in blood circulation and nutrient consumption are thrown out of balance, potentially leading to feelings of illness or fatigue. All these issues are triggered when your body is underhydrated and its fluid levels are out of balance.

So have 3-4 litres of water everyday is a must to keep yourself hydrated.

RULE 4

NO ALCOHOL,

NO CHEAT MEALS


Absolutely no alcohol or cheat meals are allowed. In order to complete the 75 Hard Challenge, and achieve your transformation goal, you must have zero alcohol for 75 days.

It doesn't specify what counts as a cheat meal since you set your own diet plan. So you can set your own rules about what is allowed and what isn't, but don't cheat yourself.

You can switch to healthier non- alcoholic alternatives, such as fruit smoothies, if you really want to include something in your day.
Fix the rules in advance and stick to them, dont change them mid challenge. Breaking of any rule is non-negotiable.

RULE 5

READ 10 PAGES DAILY


Read atleast 10 pages of any motivational, non-fiction novel. This will help you gain more knowledge. Mental growth is equally important.

The book will be of your choice and should aim at personal development. Audiobooks are not allowed.

RULE 6

PROGRESS PICTURE


Click a picture of yourself everyday for 75 days and save them here with us. No, skipping the picture is not an option.
The main aim of this is for you to look at your own progress.

If you fail to complete any one of the above tasks on any day, you will have to start from day 1 again.
